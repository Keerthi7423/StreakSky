1. OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------------------------------

StreakSky is a mobile habit tracking app that combines daily
habit management, goal setting (weekly, monthly, career),
streak tracking, GitHub-style heatmaps, rich graphs, a unique
Habit Weather System, yearly automatic goal rollover, and
daily motivational quotes from the world's greatest minds —
all powered by an on-device AI agent using Ollama.

The app makes habit building feel human, emotional, long-term,
and deeply motivating. It doesn't reset your progress at the
end of a year — it carries your momentum forward, builds on it,
and shows you the full arc of your life as a series of streaks,
weather patterns, and growth milestones.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2. APP NAME RATIONALE
----------------------------------------------------------------

Name       : StreakSky
Why        : "Streak" ties directly to the core habit mechanic.
             "Sky" connects to the weather system and conveys
             openness, infinite growth, and long-term vision.
             Together it feels fresh, mobile-first, and timeless.
             The sky never resets — it just changes. That is
             exactly how StreakSky treats your habits year
             after year.

3. TARGET USERS
----------------------------------------------------------------

4. YEARLY AUTOMATIC SYSTEM
----------------------------------------------------------------

This is one of StreakSky's most powerful and rare features.
Most apps reset everything on January 1st. StreakSky does not.
Instead it runs a fully automatic year-end and year-start
engine that preserves momentum, reviews growth, and sets up
the next year intelligently.

4.1 YEAR-END REVIEW (AUTO-GENERATED — DECEMBER 31)
----------------------------------------------------
On December 31st at 11:00 PM, StreakSky automatically
generates a full Year in Review for the user. No manual
effort required. It includes:

  HABIT REPORT CARD
  - Every habit tracked that year with:
      Total days completed
      Completion rate %
      Longest streak
      Best month
      Worst month
      Trend (improving / declining / steady)

  WEATHER YEAR SUMMARY
  - Total sunny days (all habits done)
  - Total stormy days (0 habits done)
  - Most common weather type of the year
  - Best weather month of the year
  - "Your sky was sunny 187 days this year. That is 51%
    of the year. Your best year yet."

  GOAL COMPLETION SUMMARY
  - Weekly goals: X of Y completed
  - Monthly goals: X of Y completed
  - Career goal phases: milestones hit vs missed
  - Overall goal completion score (0-100)

  STREAK HALL OF FAME
  - Top 3 longest streaks of the year with habit name
  - Number of streak shields used
  - Comeback count (how many times rebuilt a streak)
  - Total consecutive perfect weeks

  AI YEAR NARRATIVE
  - Ollama AI writes a short personal narrative paragraph
    summarizing the year based on data:
    "2025 was your most consistent year yet. You built
    a 47-day reading streak in March, weathered a storm
    in July, and made a stunning comeback in September.
    Your sky was mostly cloudy early on but turned sunny
    by Q4. Carry that momentum into 2026."

  SHAREABLE YEAR CARD
  - Beautiful visual card generated automatically
  - Shows: year, sunny day count, longest streak, top habit,
    weather emoji summary strip (12 monthly icons)
  - Exportable as image — shareable on Instagram / WhatsApp

4.2 YEAR-START SETUP (AUTO — JANUARY 1)
-----------------------------------------
On January 1st, StreakSky does NOT wipe data. Instead:

  HABIT ROLLOVER
  - All active habits automatically carry forward
  - Streaks that survived Dec 31 continue unbroken
  - User gets a prompt: "Keep all habits? Archive any?"
  - Option to adjust frequency for the new year

  GOAL ROLLOVER
  - Incomplete career goal phases roll forward automatically
  - New monthly and weekly goals are suggested by AI based
    on last year's completion patterns:
    "You completed reading goals 10/12 months last year.
    Want to raise the target this year?"
  - User approves, adjusts, or replaces AI suggestions

  NEW YEAR INTENTION PROMPT
  - AI asks 3 simple questions on Jan 1:
    1. "What is the one habit you want to master this year?"
    2. "What career milestone do you want to hit by Dec?"
    3. "What do you want your year to feel like?"
  - Answers are stored and referenced throughout the year
    in AI nudges and mid-year check-ins

  FRESH HEATMAP LAYER
  - New year opens a fresh heatmap grid (365 cells)
  - Previous years are archived and viewable
  - Year selector lets users compare 2024 vs 2025 vs 2026
    side by side

4.3 MID-YEAR AUTO CHECK-IN (JULY 1)
--------------------------------------
Halfway through the year, StreakSky auto-generates a
Mid-Year Report:

  - Pace check: "At current rate, you will complete 68%
    of your monthly goals by December."
  - Habit health score: improving / plateauing / declining
  - AI adjustment suggestions: "Consider dropping 'Cold
    shower' — 14% completion. Replace with something
    lighter to rebuild momentum."
  - Career goal projection update

4.4 MULTI-YEAR LEGACY VIEW
-----------------------------
After 2+ years of use, a Legacy View unlocks:

  - Multi-year heatmap: see 2024, 2025, 2026 stacked
  - Year-over-year comparison graph: completion rate per year
  - "Your best year was 2025 — 203 sunny days"
  - Longest streak across all years highlighted
  - Personal records board:
      Most habits in a single day: 6 (March 14, 2025)
      Longest streak ever: 73 days — Reading (2025)
      Most sunny days in a year: 203 (2025)
      Most comebacks in a year: 4 (2024)
  - AI Legacy Narrative: written summary of growth across
    all years combined

5. STREAKS
----------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 STREAK TYPES
-----------------
Every habit has its own streak. Plus an overall daily streak.

  🔥 Active Streak     — Consecutive days habit completed
  ❄️  Broken Streak     — Streak ended, shown in grey
  🛡️  Shield Active     — Missed day forgiven by shield
  🌈  Comeback Streak   — Rebuilt after a break
  👑  Legendary Streak  — Streak over 100 days
  ⚡ Lightning Streak  — 7-day perfect streak (all habits)
  💎 Diamond Streak    — 365-day streak (one full year)

5.2 STREAK MECHANICS
---------------------
  - Current streak per habit shown on habit card
  - Longest streak ever shown below current streak
  - Overall streak: all habits done in a day = full day
  - Partial streak: 60%+ of habits = soft day (no break,
    shown in different shade on heatmap)
  - Streak freeze at midnight: 1 hour grace period until
    1:00 AM for night owls

5.3 STREAK SHIELD
------------------
  - Earn 1 shield per completed 7-day streak
  - Max 3 shields stored at once
  - Use a shield to protect streak on a missed day
  - Shield shown as icon on habit card when active
  - AI reminds: "You have 2 streak shields. Rough day?
    Use one and protect your 21-day run."

5.4 COMEBACK SCORE
-------------------
  - If a 30-day streak is broken and rebuilt to 30+,
    earn a Comeback Badge
  - Comeback count tracked per year and all-time
  - Comeback streaks shown in gold on heatmap
  - AI celebrates comebacks: "Rainbow incoming. You
    rebuilt your reading streak. That took grit."

5.5 STREAK MILESTONES
----------------------
  Milestone badges unlocked automatically:
  - 7 days    → 🌱 Seedling
  - 14 days   → 🌿 Sprout
  - 21 days   → 🌳 Tree
  - 30 days   → 🔥 On Fire
  - 60 days   → ⚡ Charged
  - 90 days   → 💪 Unstoppable
  - 180 days  → 🌟 Star
  - 365 days  → 💎 Diamond
  - 500 days  → 👑 Legend
  Each milestone triggers a celebratory animation and
  a special quote from a great thinker (see Section 7).

5.6 STREAK LEADERBOARD (PERSONAL)
-----------------------------------
  - Personal best board — no social comparison pressure
  - Shows your own all-time top 5 streaks by habit
  - Updated automatically when a record is broken
  - AI acknowledges broken records:
    "New personal record. 48 days of meditation.
    Your previous best was 31. You just rewrote yourself."

----------

  - Monthly completion % line graph

  - Year-over-year comparison (multi-year)

7.5 HABIT WEATHER SYSTEM
-------------------------
  ☀️  Sunny         — All habits done
  ⛅  Partly Cloudy — 60-99% done
  🌥️  Cloudy         — 40-59% done
  🌧️  Rainy          — Under 40% done
  ⛈️  Storm          — 0% for 3+ days
  🌈  Rainbow        — First good day after storm
  🌪️  Tornado        — Longest streak broken today
