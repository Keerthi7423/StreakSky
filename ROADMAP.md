# StreakSky ROADMAP (v3.0 Feature Backlog)

## Q3 - Growth & Social Features
- **Social Accountability Groups**: Form groups of up to 5 friends to share streaks and habits.
- **Challenge Mode**: 30-day shared challenges with leaderboards.
- **Advanced Gamification**: Unlockable app themes based on career phase completion.

## Q4 - Integrations & Ecosystem
- **Apple Health & Google Fit Integration**: Automatically complete physical habits (e.g., "Walk 10k steps").
- **Wear OS / watchOS Companion App**: Quickly log habits directly from your wrist.
- **Notion Integration**: Export habit logs and journal entries to Notion databases.

## Future / Moonshots
- **AI Personalized Habit Generation**: Let Sky AI build a custom morning routine based on your goals and past performance.
- **Web Dashboard**: A full React-based web dashboard for deep data analysis on desktop.
- **API Access**: Developer API for pro users to build their own integrations.
